Robinson Crusoe Adventure Cruise

Fast boat tour from Praslin to Curieuse and St. Pierre Islands

Visit St. Pierre Island, Curieuse Island, Anse Jose, Anse Georgette and meet the Giant Tortoises; BBQ Lunch included!

Included in this adventure
  • Hotel pick-up/drop-off (Praslin hotels)
  • Landing Fees on Curieuse Island
  • Bottled water
  • BBQ Lunch

What to bring:Light casual clothing, comfortable walking shoes, swimwear, hat, sunglasses, sun cream, camera, beach towel, cash for beverages, snorkelling equipment.

Note:This tour is not recommended for pregnant women and the physically challenged. Infants under 2 years of age are not permitted.

Details

Follow in the footsteps of Robinson Crusoe with a fun-filled day of adventure, with snorkelling at St. Pierre Island, the giant tortoises of Curieuse Island Reserve, a swim at picturesque Anse Georgette Beach and a scenic cruise along the North East Coast of Praslin Island. After transfer from your Praslin hotel to Cote D'Or beach, board the fast boat and head over to St. Pierre Island, located just North of Praslin, for some swimming or snorkelling and, of course, picture taking.

Then cruise over to the Curieuse Island Reserve, where you'll get to meet the Giant Aldabra tortoises. Enjoy a one-hour guided tour of the island through the mountains and mangroves, ending at Anse Jose beach. Continue the hike to Baie Laraie, where a sumptuous BBQ lunch will be served on the beach. After lunch, return on board and head to the stunning Anse Georgette Beach for a refreshing swim and photo opportunities. End the day with a scenic cruise along the Praslin's northeast coast back to Cote D'Or beach. From here, you'll be transferred back to your Praslin hotel.

Feel free to contact us if anything remains unclear, and we'd be more than happy to help you out.
